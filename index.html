SRM GPA Calculator

So the end-sem result just came in and you have all your grades and credits in front of your your screen but you, well being you, are just too lazy to pick up a calculator and start multiplying credits and grades and get the final CGPA. Well here you can type in your grades and credit for the subject and it will calculate your final CGPA.

How to use?

For each unit of study :

  1. Enter Subject Code
  2. Enter Number of Credits
  3. Enter Grade
